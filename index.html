Timesheet

Timesheet is an affordable and easy-to-use web-based timesheet solution for your company. It allows management to better manage timesheet capturing, approvals and reporting.

Integrate this system as your company’s time tracking feature now!

Features

Web based interface

Timesheet is delivered on a web platform, accessible from both desktop and laptop. Timesheet is also ideal for a mobile workforce using smartphones with its volatile interface.

Anywhere, anytime

Timesheet is 100% web enabled, which means all users can logon to the site anytime and anywhere there is an Internet connection on all devices.

Deploy immediately

There are none of the expensive infrastructure, installation, setup or maintenance costs associated with Timesheet. You can be up and running with Timesheet in no time.

Easy time entry

Hassle-free time entry solution for your employees. Users can just log in and enter/edit the timesheet entries without needing to install anything on their local machine or even smartphones. No training required for general users.

  • Selection of jobs for time records
  • Selection of tasks for time records
  • Flexible date and time entry formats
  • Customisable entries
  • Copy time records from one day to the next

Selective access based on role permissions

Role based permission varies for administrators, managers and users. For example, managers and administrators have access to logs and timesheets approval page whereas users can only enter and edit their timesheet entries.

iPayroll integration

Does your business need the integration of your timesheets with your pay roll system? Timesheet allows the generation of iPayroll for specific employee for the dates you select.

System reporting

The following reports can be generated for your business:

  • Total hours by job (client project) and work type
  • Total hours per week per user per job (client project)
  • Leave usage
  • Total hours by user
  • Total hours by user for a week/fortnight
  • Total hours per job (client project)

Informative dashboard

Dashboard report available to administrators and managers include statistic such as number of users in the system, number of new years within the last week, number of timesheets approved in the last week and number of pending timesheets.

Messaging system

Messages can be sent and replied within the system between users. Automated messages are sent to employees when timesheets are approved.
